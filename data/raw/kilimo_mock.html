Public Service Announcement: Fall Armyworm Control Campaign

Published: July 2026 | Category: Plant Protection

The State Department for Agriculture wishes to advise all maize farmers across the country regarding the containment of Fall Armyworm (FAW) outbreaks in the current cropping season.

Recommended Mitigation Practices:

To secure crop yields, farmers are urged to adhere to the following plant health guidelines:

  • Early Detection: Monitor crops twice a week starting immediately after seedling emergence.
  • Cultural Control: Keep fields free of weeds, carry out crop rotation, and plant early to escape peak infestation.
  • Chemical Application: Apply only recommended, registered biopesticides during the early stages of infestation (first to third instar larvae).
  • Reporting: Report any severe or persistent outbreaks to the nearest sub-county agricultural extension officer.

Advisory: Distribution of Subsidized Fertilizer (Phase 3)

Under the National Agricultural Input Subsidy Program, the Ministry announces the rollout of subsidized fertilizer for the upcoming planting season.

Registered farmers can access inputs at designated National Cereals and Produce Board (NCPB) depots. To check eligibility and register, use the USSD code *882# from your mobile device.
